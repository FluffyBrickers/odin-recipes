Ingredients~

  • Somehow get yourself to the point where you have a potato in the shape of a waffle?
  • Canned Gravy?
  • A plate

Steps~

  1. Either get a pre-packaged frozen potato waffle, defrost it etc. OR just flatten out, poke some holes into any potato substitute any other potato product out there.
  2. Fry that shit up in a pan with a drizzle of oil on medium high heat for probably 4-5 minutes on both sides.
  3. Take out that bad boi of canned gravy and either heat it over a stove top in a saucepan for 8-9 minutes on medium heat til boiling, occasionally stirring; or just pop it in a bowl to microwave it for about a minute-minute half.
  4. plate on a plate like salt-bae or ramsay and enjoy something-
